Cooking pasta well is mostly about timing and salt. Bring a large pot of
water to a rolling boil, salt it generously so it tastes like the sea,
and only then add the pasta. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking, and
start tasting a minute or two before the package time is up. Reserve a
cup of the starchy cooking water before draining; a splash of it helps
bind sauce to noodles far better than plain water ever could.
